No. We handle 100% of the technical setup. You only need to provide your clinic information and we build everything. You just receive leads on your phone.
Does the bot really speak Arabic? ▼
Yes. The bot is fully configured in both Arabic and English. It automatically detects which language the patient uses and replies in the same language. It is tuned for Gulf Arabic.
How does the bot get installed on my website? ▼
We give your web developer one small line of code to paste into your website. If you do not have a developer, we handle it directly ourselves. You do nothing technical.
What if a patient asks something the bot cannot answer? ▼
The bot automatically collects the patient name and WhatsApp number and sends you an instant notification so you can follow up personally. No patient inquiry is ever lost.

Yes. There is no long-term contract. Cancel with 30 days written notice at any time. The setup fee is a one-time investment and the monthly retainer is fully flexible.

Yes. Patient data collected by the bot is sent only to you. We never store, sell, or share any patient information with third parties. Full confidentiality is guaranteed in our service contract.

Yes but it is free. WhatsApp Business is a free app from WhatsApp. If you do not have it yet we will guide you through setting it up in 10 minutes before connecting the bot.
